🎮 Game Features

🏢
30+ Floors

Progressive difficulty from tutorial floors to chaotic endgame. Each floor introduces new mechanics.

🌦️
Dynamic Weather

Sunny, rain, storm, snow, fog — conditions change mid-game and affect gameplay mechanics.

⚡
Power-ups

Speed, Jump, Shield, Invincibility, Double Coins & XP — pickups that change your strategy.

⚔️
PvP System

Arena combat with kill tracking, rankings, and rewards. Fight other players to climb the leaderboard.

👑
Rank System

From Civilian to Owner — 7 ranks. Earn prestige through gameplay and climb the hierarchy.

🔄
Prestige

Complete the game, earn a prestige point, restart with bigger rewards and harder challenges.
